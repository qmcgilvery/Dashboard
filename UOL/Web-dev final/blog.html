Our next event is fast approaching! Sign up soon to test your fitness and have a chance to win one of our prizes. First place gets a free month of unlimited classes, second place gets one week and third place gets a free personal training session! All prizes are awarded to mens, women’s and age group categories.

The structure of this event is a 1000m ski, followed by 2000m on the bike bike then a 1000m row and finished off with a 2000m run, but that’s just one round! You’ll have to complete two more rounds to finish! It may seem like a lot but it should take around an hour and if you’ve been following along with our training plan then it should be a walk in the park ;)

So sign-up today! Registration closes 09/30
